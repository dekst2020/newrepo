The Best Comic and Anime Apps for Super Hero Fans

Apps By Khaled Shariar

-

Mar 6, 2020 Facebook Twitter Pinterest WhatsApp Linkedin

Comic and anime culture is available at your fingertips with this selection of Android apps!

We can do virtually everything on our smartphone and tablet devices these days, and there’s something for everyone to enjoy. The world of comics, cartoons , and anime culture is hugely popular and always evolving. For example, rather than requiring a physical comic book in your hand, comics are now available digitally instead, and some folk enjoys building their electronic collections. This means that the use of comic reader apps has grown, and there are also other apps that will help you to create your own impressive comic works or stream anime online.

The portability of Android devices means you can now have this whole arena at your fingertips, and it’s a great way of having instant access to tens of thousands of offerings as well as keeping in touch with all the latest. The Google Play Store is awash with comic and animation apps so if this subject is something that interests you there are many apps that you will get a lot of pleasure from. However, like most topics, there’s a fair share of these apps that are not so good and worth avoiding.

Because of this, we’ve done the legwork and have put together our choice of the 10 best comic and anime apps for Android. Most of these recommended apps are free to download and use although some contain in-app purchases for additional features and more. Some of the free apps also offer a premium version as a trial period, after which you can choose whether to upgrade or not.

These comic and anime apps cover a wide range, so whatever your specific interest you should find at least one or two that will be excellent options for you.

10 best comic book readers for Android

1. COMICS – COMIXOLOGY

Price: Free

This app has a whole lot to offer and whether your preference is Marvel Comics, DC Comics, Image, Manga, or Dark Horse, you’ll find all of these covered along with plenty of others. The app includes a 30-Day Free Trial of ComiXology Unlimited (for US customers only), which is a subscription service with a library of thousands upon thousands of digital comics, manga, and graphic novels to enjoy. This content includes the likes of Guardians of the Galaxy , Peanuts, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Attack on Titan, and Saga.

The app acts as a digital comics hub so you can easily access all of the titles you have on your device or buy books in-app. Features include SD card support so you never need to worry about using all of the space on your device, offline reading, and guided view reading which is particularly good for small screen use.

Download Comics

2. CRUNCHYROLL

Price: Free with ads

Think Netflix for Anime and you won’t go far wrong. Crunchyroll is a legal way of watching the latest anime episodes as well as prior seasons of your favorites, with more than 25,000 episodes and 15,000 hours worth to choose from. Do note that this free version has limitations but does include a 14-day free trial of the Premium membership. The latter offers new episodes immediately after the Japanese TV screenings as well as ad-free watching in HD quality, A few examples of available content are Blue Exorcist, Hunter X Hunter, Fullmetal Alchemist, Samurai Champloo, and JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure.

We did find this app a bit glitchy from time to time but not enough to deter us from using it, so why not check it out.

Download Crunchyroll

3. HOW TO DRAW ANIME – DRAWSHOW

Price: Free with ads/ Offers IAP

If your focus is on making your own comics and anime, this is a great drawing app to help you along the way as it covers various anime styles such as American comics, Japanese manga, and European comics. DrawShow is suitable for beginners right through to skilled artists, with guides that cover drafting animations, drawing step-by-step, coloring and more, as well as categories such as figures, animals and many others. Just some of the drawing tools available are pencils, brushes, and lacing pens, and further features include multiple layer support, an assortment of materials, vector zooming, and offline support.

We particularly like the community aspect of this app that allows you to engage with other anime fans. Additionally, you can submit your own creations and the best on the monthly billboard can receive prizes. The tutorials are available from both professional artists and fanart enthusiasts and there’s even a special billboard for those new to DrawShow. After spending some time with this app we can endorse the 4.5/5 star user rating that the app receives.

Download DrawShow

4. COMICAT

Price: $2.99

This is an excellent comic reader app that is well suited for those who want to be able to manage their catalog. It’s packed with features and includes support for formats that include CBZ, CBR, PDF, CB7, CBT, Zip, Rar, 7z and more. The app makes it very easy and quick to build your catalog in one handy place. It scans your device and all of your comics are collected on a virtual bookshelf from various sources. These include your device storage, SD card, cloud, and download folder.

Comicat has an appealing user interface, multiple themes for its bookshelf, password protection , multiple view modes, gesture controls, image enhancer, and plenty more to admire. Although it’s a paid app, the developers state that it will have lifelong free updates with further improvements and fresh features.

Download ComiCat

5. WEBCOMICS

Price: Free with ads/ Offers IAP

WebComics bills itself as an “online gathering place for comics and manga”, with genres that include action comics, fantasy comics, horror, comedy, and BL comics among others. It’s a licensed comic reading platform with 1080p HD comic strips, and it also offers community and comments so you can engage with other fans, share your ideas and creations, and keep in touch with all of the latest developments. We found the app offered very speedy loading and as a bonus, there are new chapters released every day. Although in-app purchases are available there is plenty to enjoy without having to splash your cash.

Download WebComics

6. ANIME AVATAR CREATOR: MAKE YOUR OWN AVATAR

Price: Free with ads

Hands up! Who has been tempted to create an anime avatar of themselves? I know I have and if you’d like to do it too, this app makes it as easy as falling off a log. Okay, so it’s not as serious as some other anime apps but it’s a lot of fun playing around and perfecting an extreme version of what you think you look like. There’s an assortment of templates to use, and apart from choosing the basics such as hair, eyes, and mouth, it’s possible to make different facial expressions with the right combinations.

It’s totally free to download and use, and when you’re done you can share your anime avatar online or save it to your gallery.

Download Anime Avatar Creator

7. VRV: ANIME, GAME VIDEOS & MORE

Price: Free with ads

Anyone interested in watching anime should take a look at this VRV streaming service. It covers a wide range of content such as anime, tech, and sci-fi, and just a few of the choices are Funimation, Geek & Sundry, Mondo, Cartoon Hangover, Shudder, MUBI, and Rooter Teeth. You’ll be able to watch on your mobile device whenever you choose, create your own watchlist, and interact with the community. If you like the app you can use subscribe with a digital purchase to the premium version. For that, you can get rid of the ads and take advantage of offline viewing.

Download VRV: Anime

8. MADEFIRE: COMICS & MOTION BOOKS

Price: Free/ Offers IAP

Digital storytelling and the latest content available is the name of the game with Madefire, with 360 panoramic views, sound, motion, and interactivity. It’s an intuitive app that provides an excellent and immersive reading experience with free content available. There is an abundant catalog of digital comics and motion books and as a mere taster of what you can enjoy there’s the DC Universe, Star Trek, Transformers, Star Trek, Dark Horse, Oni Press, and Top Cow for starters. You can also browse new print titles and use in-app purchases at the Print Books store to buy same-day comic releases.

Download Madefire Comics

9. TAPAS – COMICS, NOVELS, AND STORIES

Price: Free with ads/ Offers IAP

Tapas has a user-friendly interface and offers a vast amount of comics and web novels. There are bestsellers as well as stories from amateur authors, and the collections include fantasy, comedy and more. It runs on a try before you buy scheme so you can get free samples of stories and earn coins and keys to unlock further episodes, or use in-app purchases. Like several of our best comic and anime apps for Android, this one has a community aspect, and a further feature is the ability to download episodes to read offline.

Download Tapas

10. ANIME & MANGA AMINO FOR OTAKUS

Price: Free

The last of our choices are a little different, as it’s a social network aimed purely at anime and manga superfans. If you want to be in touch with people who share your love for all things anime, this is the platform you’ve been looking for. It has a young feel and on the Google Play Store, it has attained a very high rating of 4.7/5 stars.

Anime and cosplay enthusiasts across the world can easily engage in their favorite subject and make friends with those who are equally passionate about anime. Because of this, it provides a terrific way of sharing your experiences of conventions and events (especially when family and friends don’t share your interest).

Download Anime & Manga Amino

Wrap up

Over recent years, comic and anime culture has become increasingly popular and the attention isn’t likely to fade away any time soon. Our pick of the 10 best comic and anime apps for Android offers just a small selection of the large variety available. With this in mind, we’ve tried to include different aspects such as reading, watching, and creating in order to appeal to a wide variety of enthusiasts. Do remember that new comic and anime apps are continually arriving on the Google Play Store so if you spot a new one that you feel is worth mentioning, drop us a comment in the usual way.
